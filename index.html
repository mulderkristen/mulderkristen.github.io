Space Prison Escape!

You are a prisoner on an alien vessel. The power has failed, opening your cell door. Now you have an opportunity to escape!

  • There are 25 doors in the pod bay, but only one is unlocked.
  • You only have time to check 20 doors before the alien guards find you.
  • Be warned: one of the doors is faulty and will suck you directly into space!
  • Good luck -- you'll need it if you hope to return to Earth!
